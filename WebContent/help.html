How To Get Started?

Signing Up

Step 1: Go To Home Page And Click On Sign-Up button

    Register Page
  1. Enter Your Details.
  2. Submit.

Step 2: Login To Your Account By Clicking At Sign-In

  1. Enter Credentials You Used While Signing-up.
  2. Login.
  3. You Will Be Taken To Dashboard

Personal Account:

  1. Add Your Monthly Expense
    1. Find Personal Expense On The Left Panel(On Computer) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile with Icon ☰ on Top Left Corner).
    2. Fill in the Amount and Description and click on Submit.
  2. Add Monthly Income
    1. Find Add Income On The Left Panel(On Computer) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile with Icon ☰ on Top Left Corner).
    2. Fill in the Amount and Description and click on Submit.
  3. Generate Reports

    ♯ To Generate Income Statement Click On "Earned" Panel On The Dashboard

    1. Income / Expense Reports
    2. Report Type - Daily/Monthly

    ♯ To Generate Expense Statement Click On "Spent" Panel On The Dashboard

    1. Income / Expense Reports
    2. Report Type - Daily/Monthly

    ♯ For Your Balance Calculation

    1. Click On Balance Panel
    2. A model will open describing your earning and your expenditures so far.

Group Operation:

  1. Create a Group
    1. Near The Name On Right Corner(On Large devices like tablet or pc) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile, Next to your name) with icon ▾.
    2. Click On create group.
    3. Enter the group name.
    4. Click on Submit.
    5. Conformation Text Appears.
    6. A request is sent to Admin.
  2. Join a Group
    1. Near The Name On Right Corner(On Large devices like tablet or pc) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile, Next to your name) with icon ▾.
    2. Click On Join Group.
    3. Enter the group ID.
    4. Click on Submit.
    5. Conformation Text Appears.
    6. A request is sent to Group Head.
  3. Manage Groups You Created

    ♯ Find "Head Of" tab (Under The Groups tab) On The Left Panel(On Large devices like tablet or pc) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile, with Icon ☰ on Top Left Corner).

    1. Here You will see the list of all the groups you are head of
    2. You can delete the group from here.
      Deleting the group will remove all the members from Group and delete all data related to group(including transaction history and dues).
    3. If you click on droup id, you will see the list of all the members in your group
  4. Manage Groups You Joined

    ♯ Find "Member Of" tab (Under The Groups tab) On The Left Panel(On Large devices like tablet or pc) Or Drop-Down List(On Mobile, with Icon ☰ on Top Left Corner).

    1. Here You will see the list of all the groups you are member of.
    2. If you click on droup id, you will be taken to dashboard of that group.

  5. For Group Statement

    1. Click On Group Statement
    2. A Detailed statement will appear depending on the number of days you choose.

  6. To Add a bill For the group

    1. Click On Add New Bill
    2. Enter The Description And Amount.
    3. Click On Add New Bill

    Your Bill will be registered as pending and will be sent to all members of that group for polling.
    It will be accepted if number of votes are more than or equal to 50% else rejected.
    Once accepted the amount will be reflected in every member's account

  7. To poll for the bill in the group

    1. Pending bills on the group dashboard panel
    2. Click on accept or reject.

    Your Response will be registered
    The bill will be accepted if number of votes are more than or equal to 50% else rejected.
    Once accepted the amount will be reflected in every member's account
